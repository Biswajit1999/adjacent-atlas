# OG cover — generation prompt

For a 1200×630 social/open-graph image. A code-free alternative to the generated
SVG card in `scripts/export-social-card.ts`.

> A dark observatory scene, 1200×630, near-black background (#0b0f14) with a
> faint teal radial wash in the upper right. A sparse 3D field of glowing points
> in teal, blue, amber, violet, green, and rose, connected by thin low-opacity
> lines, drifting toward a slightly brighter centre. Upper-left: the wordmark
> "Adjacent Atlas" in white Inter 600, with the tagline "An observatory for the
> adjacent possible" beneath in muted grey. Restrained, scientific, lots of
> negative space. No UI chrome, no charts, no people.

Keep text legible against the field; reserve the left third for type.
